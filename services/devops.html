DevOps & Infrastructure

CI/CD pipelines, cloud architecture, and Infrastructure as Code that let your team ship with confidence.

The Problem I Solve

Manual deployments, inconsistent environments, and infrastructure that only one person understands — these are the silent killers of engineering velocity. When your deployment process is fragile, everything slows down: feature delivery, bug fixes, and developer morale.

I help teams build infrastructure and deployment systems that are reliable, repeatable, and don't require tribal knowledge to operate.

What I Do

  • CI/CD pipeline design — Automated build, test, and deploy pipelines that catch problems before they reach production
  • Infrastructure as Code — Reproducible environments using Terraform, so your infrastructure is versioned and reviewable like any other code
  • Cloud architecture — Right-sized AWS and Azure setups that balance performance, reliability, and cost
  • Containerization — Docker-based workflows that eliminate "works on my machine" problems
  • Monitoring and alerting — Observability setups so your team knows about problems before your users do
  • Developer experience — Onboarding scripts, documentation, and tooling that get new developers productive fast

How I Work

I bill hourly and work directly with your team. I focus on building systems that your team can operate and extend independently. Every infrastructure change is documented and code-reviewed, so there are no surprises after I move on.
