The Problem I Solve

Your team is spending hours on manual data processing that should be automated. Or you know AI could improve your product, but you're not sure how to go from proof-of-concept to production. The gap between a working notebook and a reliable production system is where most AI projects stall.

I help teams build data automation and ML systems that actually work in production — not just in a demo.
