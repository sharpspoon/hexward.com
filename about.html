Who I Am

Hi, I’m Robin Ward, the founder and sole developer behind Hexward. I began this journey in 2015 under the name Steel City Sites, where I focused on web design and front-end development for small businesses. Over the years, as client needs grew, so did my capabilities—leading to the creation of Hexward in 2025 as a full-stack development and consulting practice.

If you’re looking for someone who can take your idea from concept to launch—or step in to untangle an existing system—I can help. Whether it’s building full-stack web apps, setting up cloud infrastructure, streamlining data workflows, or creating custom software solutions, I work directly with clients to deliver clean, scalable results without the agency overhead. Let’s build something that works—and lasts.
